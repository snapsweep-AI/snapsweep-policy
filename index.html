Our privacy principles

Six commitments we make to every SnapSweep AI user

01
On-device first
All AI scanning runs locally using TensorFlow Lite. Your photos are never uploaded or transmitted to any server.
02
Minimum data
We collect only what is strictly necessary — anonymous counts and crash logs. Nothing more.
03
No selling
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial gain. Ever.
04
You're in control
You can opt out of analytics and change your ad consent preference at any time from the App's Settings.
05
Transparent
Every data point we collect is documented in our Privacy Policy. No hidden tracking, no secret data flows.
06
Secure by design
All data in transit is TLS-encrypted. Crash reporting is disabled in debug builds. We follow Android security best practices.
