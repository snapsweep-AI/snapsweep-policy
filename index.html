Privacy Policy – SnapSweep AI

Last Updated: 2026

SnapSweep AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

SnapSweep AI is designed to clean and organize your device storage. Core processing happens locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Photos or videos are NOT uploaded to our servers
  • No personal media content is stored externally

3. On-Device Processing

All image analysis and classification are performed locally on your device using AI models.

4. Data We Collect

  • App Activity: Feature usage, interactions
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs (Crashlytics)

5. How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Optimize features
  • Provide relevant ads

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash reporting)
  • Google Play Billing (Payments)
  • Google Drive (Optional backup)

7. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with third-party services listed above for analytics and advertising purposes.

8. Google Drive Backup

Files are uploaded only if you explicitly choose backup. We do not access or store your files otherwise.

9. Data Retention

We do not store user media. Analytics data is retained as per Firebase policies.

10. User Control

  • You can revoke permissions anytime
  • You can disable ads personalization via device settings
  • You can uninstall the app anytime

11. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect user data.

12. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

13. International Users

Data may be processed in countries outside your region via third-party services.

14. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
