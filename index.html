Privacy Policy – SnapSweep AI

SnapSweep AI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Photo Access

SnapSweep AI requires access to your photos to analyze and clean your gallery. All scanning is performed locally on your device.

2. Google Drive Backup

If you choose to use backup features, selected files may be uploaded to your Google Drive. This is user-initiated.

3. Data Collection

We collect limited data for analytics and app improvement using Firebase Analytics.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve ads.

5. Purchases

Premium purchases are handled via Google Play Billing. We do not store payment data.

6. Data Security

Your photos remain on your device unless you explicitly use backup features.

7. Contact

Email: support@snapsweep.ai
