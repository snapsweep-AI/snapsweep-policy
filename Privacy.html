02

What We Never Collect Important

We want to be crystal clear about what SnapSweep AI does not collect:

  • Photos, videos, audio, or any media file content
  • File names, folder names, or file paths on your device
  • Your contacts, messages, call logs, or location data
  • Your name, email address, or any account information (unless you choose to sign in with Google)
  • Device identifiers such as IMEI, phone number, or MAC address
  • Clipboard contents, keystrokes, or screen content
  • Any data from other apps installed on your device
03

How Data Is Used

The limited data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • App improvement: Anonymous usage events help us understand which features are most valuable and where to focus development
  • Bug fixing: Crash reports allow us to identify and resolve issues affecting specific devices or Android versions
  • Performance optimisation: Scan duration metrics help us make the AI model faster for all users
  • Feature gating: Purchase status is used solely to unlock Premium features you have paid for
  • Legal compliance: Consent records are retained to demonstrate compliance with GDPR and CCPA obligations

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose beyond what is stated above.

04

On-Device AI Processing AI

The core of SnapSweep AI — scanning and classifying your photos — happens entirely on your device using TensorFlow Lite / LiteRT, Google's on-device machine learning runtime.

🔒
Your photos never leave your phone. The AI model is downloaded to your device during installation and runs locally. No photo, thumbnail, or file content is transmitted to our servers or any third party during scanning.
  • The AI model file (model.tflite) is bundled with the App — no internet connection is needed to scan
  • Classification results (e.g. "duplicate", "blurry") are generated and stored on-device only
  • Anonymous signals used to improve the model (e.g. scan count, items deleted count) contain no file content
  • Model updates are delivered as part of App updates via Google Play — not over the air separately

Advertising & Consent

The free version of SnapSweep AI displays ads served by Google AdMob. On first launch, you will be presented with a consent screen (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) to choose your ad personalisation preference.

ℹ
You can change your ad consent preference at any time by going to Settings → Ad Preferences within the App.
  • Personalised ads: If you consent, AdMob may use your advertising ID and app interactions to show more relevant ads
  • Non-personalised ads: If you decline, you will still see ads but they will not be based on your interests or past behaviour
  • Premium users: No ads are shown. AdMob is not initialised for Premium users
  • We do not control the content of ads served by Google AdMob
  • You can opt out of interest-based advertising at the device level via Android Settings → Google → Ads
07

Google Drive & Sign-In

SnapSweep AI offers an optional Google Drive Backup feature. This feature requires you to sign in with your Google account.

☁
Google Drive integration is entirely optional and user-initiated. We only access the specific files you choose to back up — we do not have access to the rest of your Google Drive.
  • We request only the minimum Google Drive permission scope needed for backup functionality
  • Files backed up to Google Drive are stored in your Google account — not on SnapSweep AI servers
  • We do not read, analyse, or retain copies of files you back up to Drive
  • You can revoke Google Drive access at any time via your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Signing out of Google within the App immediately revokes our access to your Drive

Your Rights GDPR / CCPA

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Right to restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object: Object to our processing of your data for analytics purposes
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw ad consent at any time in App Settings
  • California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@snapsweep.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • All data transmitted to Firebase services uses TLS encryption in transit
  • Firebase access is restricted using service account credentials and security rules
  • The App uses Android Keystore for any locally stored sensitive preferences
  • We regularly review third-party SDK versions for security vulnerabilities
  • Crashlytics is disabled in debug builds to prevent development data contaminating production

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to support@snapsweep.ai.
